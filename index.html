TOP 5 LUNCH SPOTS

Looking for a place to quickly grab lunch in Athens, Ohio? Here are five of my favorite lunch spots in the area.

Bagel Street Deli

27 S Court St, Athens, OH 45701

Bagel Street Deli puts its own twist on traditional deli sandwiches by turning them into bagelwiches. To order, you first pick a type of bagelwich, then the type of bagel and the type of cheese. Bagel Street Deli is know for serving their food wrapped in tin foil, which you can then use to sculpt something to add to their graffitied walls.

FOOD RECOMMENDATION

I always get the Tom’s Turkey Bagelwich from Bagel Street Deli, on a plain bagel with provolone cheese. This bagelwich has turkey, lettuce, tomato and mayo and is served warm wrapped in their signature tin foil wraps.

Tom’s Turkey Bagelwich from Bagel Street Deli

Brenen's

38 S Court St, Athens, OH 45701

Brenen’s vast variety of sandwiches and drinks make it a must-try spot in Athens, whether you’re looking for a quick caffeine boost or a tasty meal. With over 35 sandwiches listed on the menu, you’re bound to find something that sounds good.

FOOD RECOMMENDATION

My number one favorite sandwich from Brenen’s is the Chicken Melt. You can’t go wrong with sliced chicken paired with melted swiss cheese, lettuce, tomato and your choice of mayo or honey mustard (I recommend mayo) on toasted wheat bread.

As a vegetarian option, I recommend the Tomato & Pesto Melt panini. Tomatoes, melted muenster and basil pesto are a combination too good to pass up.

Brenen’s Chicken Melt sandwich & Deep River Mesquite BBQ Kettle Chips

Holy Guacamole

Food Truck on E Union St, Athens, OH 45701

Holy Guacamole Taco Stand is one of my favorite food trucks in the Athens area. I originally was intrigued by the punny name, but the food is what keeps me coming back. They have a variety of Mexican food, including tacos, burritos, fajitas, quesadillas and tamales. Everything is made to order from all fresh ingredients.

FOOD RECOMMENDATION

My favorite item to get from Holy Guacamole is the Chicken Quesadilla. Their quesadillas come with lettuce, fresh pica de gallo and guacamole on the side. I also get some sour cream on the side.

Holy Guacamole’s Chicken Quesadilla meal

West 82

1 Park Pl, Athens, OH 45701

West 82 is a food court on the first floor of Ohio University’s Baker University Center. It features a variety of food types, including a salad bar, a pizza place, a deli, a grill, and a made to order Mexican food station.

FOOD RECOMMENDATION

I have a couple favorites that I get often from West 82. The first is the Chicken Pesto Sandwich from the Deli. I get the sandwich on artisan wheat bread. It comes with deli chicken, provolone cheese, roasted red peppers, pesto and mayo. The deli also has an excellent Italian Sub.

The second item that I get often is the Chicken Quesadilla. I get it on a wheat tortilla shell with chicken and melted mixed cheese. I also get sour cream on the side to dip the quesadilla into.

Chicken Quesadilla from West 82

Chipotle

41 S Court St, Athens, OH 45701

You can’t go wrong with a burrito or bowl from Chipotle. Despite its long lines from being located on a college campus, Chipotle is a lunch spot that I visit frequently. I like the customization aspect and the consistency of the food.

FOOD RECOMMENDATION

My order at Chipotle is fairly simple: a burrito bowl with brown rice, chicken and cheese. Sometimes I add corn, sour cream or guacamole, but this varies from time to time! I also get a tortilla shell on the side.
